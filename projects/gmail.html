GMail

About

Gmail is a popular email service developed by Google. It's known for its user-friendly interface, powerful search capabilities, and robust security features.

With over 1.8 billion active users, Gmail is one of the most widely used email services in the world. It offers users a range of advanced features, such as customizable labels, filters, and priority inbox, as well as integration with other Google services like Google Drive and Google Calendar.

Gmail also includes a powerful spam filter and two-factor authentication to protect users' accounts. With its seamless integration with other Google services and user-friendly interface, Gmail has become a go-to email service for many people around the world.
